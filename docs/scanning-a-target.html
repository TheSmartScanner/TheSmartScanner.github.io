Scanning a target

Starting a scan in SmartScanner is easy. Just enter your website’s URL and click the “scan” button. SmartScanner automatically scans the website, and you can see the results in the dashboard. During the scan, you click pause/resume or stop the scan.

Note: once you stop a scan, it cannot be resumed.

If you need to customize scan configurations, you can do it by clicking “change scan config” below the target input field. You can select tests and also set authentication and proxy parameters for your target in the scan config page.
